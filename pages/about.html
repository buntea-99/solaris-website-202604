About Solaris Wireless

The institutional technology supply company trusted by operators, enterprises and governments worldwide

Who we are

Solaris Wireless is an institutional technology supply, provisioning and fulfilment company headquartered in Miami, Florida. Since our founding in 2016, we have built a global supply chain infrastructure designed to serve the precise, high-volume requirements of network operators, government agencies, Fortune 500 enterprises and educational institutions.

We are not a consumer retailer. Every system we operate, every relationship we maintain and every process we follow has been engineered for institutional-scale procurement. Our clients do not order one device at a time. They order thousands, and they need those devices sourced from authorised channels, configured to exact specifications, and delivered on schedule to locations across the world.

Solaris Wireless has been a Google-approved vendor since 2016, and our client roster includes some of the most recognisable names in telecommunications, technology and government. From T-Mobile and Vodafone to Amazon marketplace sellers and Pacific Island telecom operators, we serve organisations that demand reliability, compliance and scale.

What We Do

Three core capabilities, one integrated supply chain

Our operations span the full lifecycle of institutional technology procurement. From initial sourcing through to end-user delivery, Solaris Wireless manages every stage with the rigour and accountability that institutional clients require.

01

Supply & Sourcing

We source mobile devices, laptops, IoT hardware, Starlink terminals and consumer electronics through authorised distributor networks and direct OEM relationships. Our procurement team manages pricing negotiations, allocation guarantees and compliance documentation for every order, regardless of volume.

02

Device Provisioning

Our provisioning facilities handle OS flashing, SIM lock configuration, carrier branding, app pre-installation, MDM enrolment and custom firmware deployment. We have provisioned over 10,000 devices with bespoke configurations for network operators, government programmes and enterprise mobility deployments.

03

Fulfilment & Logistics

We operate direct-to-customer delivery, freight forwarding, consignment holding, kitting and retail packaging services. Our fulfilment network leverages five strategic nodes across four continents to minimise transit times and reduce shipping costs for institutional buyers.

Sectors We Serve

Built for the organisations that need it most

Government

Federal, state and municipal agencies that require compliant procurement channels, secure device provisioning and documented chain-of-custody for technology assets deployed to public employees and contractors.

Telecommunications

Mobile network operators, MVNOs and ISPs that need large-scale handset sourcing, carrier-branded provisioning and subscriber fulfilment capabilities. Our telecom clients include Vodafone, T-Mobile, Orange, Republic Wireless, Bluesky Cook Islands and Cook Islands Telecom.

Enterprise

Fortune 500 companies, technology firms and fast-growing organisations that procure devices in volume for employee mobility programmes, field operations and customer-facing deployments. We serve companies including Google, Ritual.co and Amazon marketplace sellers.

Education

School districts, universities and educational technology providers that require bulk device procurement, preconfigured learning environments, MDM enrolment and direct-to-school fulfilment at scale.

Medical

Healthcare systems, telehealth providers and medical device companies that need ruggedised hardware, HIPAA-compliant provisioning and reliable fulfilment to clinical sites, field workers and patients.

Our Journey

A decade of building institutional supply chains

From a single-city operation to a global supply chain spanning four continents, Solaris Wireless has grown by earning the trust of the world's most demanding technology buyers.

2016

Founded in Miami & Google Approval

Solaris Wireless established its headquarters in Miami, Florida, and earned approval as a Google-authorised vendor in its inaugural year. This early validation from one of the world's largest technology companies set the standard for every client relationship that followed.

2017

Telecom Operator Partnerships

Began supplying handsets and devices to mobile network operators including Republic Wireless and regional carriers. Established provisioning capabilities for carrier-branded device configurations, SIM lock management and firmware deployment.

2018

European Expansion

Opened our first international supply chain node through a strategic partnership in the Netherlands, providing European distribution capabilities and enabling us to serve clients such as Vodafone and Orange with reduced transit times and localised compliance.

2019

Asia-Pacific & Middle East Network

Expanded our global footprint with strategic partners in Singapore, Hong Kong and Dubai. This gave Solaris Wireless supply chain coverage across four continents and enabled us to serve Pacific Island telecom operators including Bluesky Cook Islands and Cook Islands Telecom.

2021

100,000 Unit Milestone

Surpassed 100,000 mobile units supplied to institutional clients worldwide. Simultaneously expanded our product portfolio to include laptops, IoT hardware, Starlink terminals and consumer electronics to meet the evolving needs of enterprise and government buyers.

2023

Enterprise & Government Scale

Deepened relationships with enterprise clients including Amazon marketplace sellers and Parallel Wireless. Expanded provisioning capacity to support 10,000+ custom-configured devices, and added government and education sector programmes to our client portfolio.

Today

Five Nodes, Four Continents, One Mission

Solaris Wireless now operates five global supply chain nodes spanning Miami, the Netherlands, Singapore, Hong Kong and Dubai. We continue to invest in provisioning technology, logistics infrastructure and client relationships to remain the institutional technology supply partner of choice for operators, enterprises and governments worldwide.

Our Values

The principles behind every shipment

Institutional clients do not tolerate ambiguity. Neither do we. Every decision at Solaris Wireless is guided by a clear set of operating principles that have earned us the trust of some of the world's most demanding organisations.

Accountability at Every Stage

From procurement to provisioning to final-mile delivery, every unit in our supply chain is tracked, documented and accounted for. Our institutional clients require full chain-of-custody visibility, and we deliver it as standard.

Scale Without Compromise

Volume does not diminish quality. Whether we are provisioning 50 devices or 5,000, every unit receives the same configuration rigour, quality assurance checks and packaging standards. Our systems are built to maintain consistency at any scale.

Global Reach, Local Precision

Five supply chain nodes across four continents allow us to operate globally while maintaining the precision and responsiveness of a local partner. We understand regional compliance requirements, import regulations and carrier-specific standards in every market we serve.

Long-Term Partnerships

We do not optimise for single transactions. Our business model is built on enduring relationships with clients who return year after year because we have earned their trust through consistent execution, transparent communication and institutional reliability.

Global Presence

Five strategic nodes across four continents

Our supply chain architecture is designed for geographic resilience and speed-to-market. Each node serves as a regional hub for sourcing, provisioning and fulfilment, reducing transit times and enabling localised compliance for institutional buyers worldwide.
