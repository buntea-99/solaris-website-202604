Zero-Touch Android Deployment: How Solaris Ships Pre-Enrolled Devices

By the Solaris Wireless Procurement Team Last updated: April 30, 2026
Quick answer

Solaris Wireless ships bulk Android devices pre-registered in your Google Zero-Touch Enrollment tenant so they auto-enrol into your MDM on first power-on. The employee unboxes, signs in with corporate credentials and the device is fully managed within 60 seconds. Custom OS, SIM lock and carrier branding are available alongside zero-touch for MVNO and field-deployment programmes.

Key facts
  • Programme: Google Zero-Touch Enrollment, registered partner
  • OEM coverage: Samsung, Motorola, Pixel, Sonim, Kyocera, HMD, Transsion
  • MDM compatibility: Intune, Workspace ONE, Jamf, MobileIron, SOTI, MaaS360, Knox Manage
  • Custom layer: Custom OS, SIM lock, branding available alongside Zero-Touch
  • Volume range: 50 to 10,000 units per batch

Why Zero-Touch Matters for Bulk Android Deployment

Solaris Wireless, founded 2013, has been serving institutional buyers in this category since the company's earliest engagements. An enterprise deploying 1,000 Android phones to employees has two operational paths. The bad path: ship every device to IT, have IT power on each device, manually enrol each device into the MDM, then re-ship to the employee. The good path: register each device IMEI in your Google Zero-Touch portal before shipment so it auto-enrols on first activation, and ship sealed direct to the employee.

Solaris Wireless operates the second path as the default. Devices arrive at the employee's desk pre-registered in your Zero-Touch tenant; the employee unboxes, powers on, signs in with corporate credentials, and the device auto-enrols to your MDM with apps and policies applied before they reach the home screen.

How Solaris's Zero-Touch Process Works

1. We confirm your Zero-Touch portal configuration

Before allocation, the Solaris account team confirms your Zero-Touch portal is configured with the correct EMM federation, enrolment profile and device-assignment policy. If your team is setting up Zero-Touch for the first time, we coordinate with your IT to validate the configuration on a 5-10 unit pilot batch before scaling.

2. We allocate handsets to your SKU mix

Solaris sources Android handsets from Samsung, Motorola, Google (Pixel), Sonim, Kyocera, HMD (Nokia) and Transsion through OEM commercial channels. For deployments that need a specific OEM mix (e.g., Samsung Galaxy S26 for executives, Motorola Edge for field staff, Sonim XP10 for ruggedised field roles), we coordinate the allocation against your assignment plan.

3. We register each IMEI in your Zero-Touch tenant

Before the device leaves the provisioning facility, we register the IMEI to your Zero-Touch tenant and the assignment profile. Devices arrive at the employee already linked to your enterprise. No manual lookup or staging required at receipt.

4. Custom OS, SIM lock and branding layered if required

For MVNO programmes and government engagements, Zero-Touch enrolment is layered alongside custom OS flashing, SIM lock to your network, splash-screen branding and SIM-to-subscriber pre-pairing. Solaris has run this combined provisioning for Republic Wireless, Pacific MVNO operators and the Ritual.co kiosk programme (10,000+ units, see the Ritual case study).

5. Direct-to-employee fulfilment with asset tags

Devices ship sealed in tamper-evident packaging with your asset-tag scheme applied (barcode, QR, RFID per your spec). Chain-of-custody tracking is available for compliance-sensitive engagements (government, healthcare, finance).

When Zero-Touch Is the Right Choice

Zero-Touch is the default for any institutional Android deployment of 100+ units. For Samsung-heavy fleets, Solaris pairs Zero-Touch with Knox Mobile Enrollment for the deeper Knox-tier configuration depth (Knox security profile, Knox container, Samsung-specific device restrictions).

For the small subset of deployments that need beyond-Zero-Touch provisioning (custom carrier OS, kiosk lockdown, splash-screen branding), Solaris layers those steps on the same engagement so the device ships fully ready, not just MDM-enrolled.

For pricing and lead time on your specific deployment, contact the Solaris team. The team responds within one business day.
