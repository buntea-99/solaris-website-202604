Bulk iPhone Supply for Enterprise IT Teams

By the Solaris Wireless Procurement Team Last updated: April 30, 2026
Quick answer

Solaris Wireless supplies bulk iPhones to enterprise IT teams as an Apple Authorised Reseller registered as the authorised purchaser in your Apple Business Manager (ABM) tenant. Each device ships sealed, auto-enrols in your MDM via DEP on first activation, and lands on the employee's desk asset-tagged and AppleCare-covered. Volume range 50 to 5,000 units per engagement.

Key facts
  • Channel: Apple Authorised Reseller
  • Provisioning: ABM auto-enrolment via DEP
  • Service: AppleCare for Business attached
  • Fulfilment: Direct-to-employee, asset-tagged
  • Volume range: 50 to 5,000 units per engagement

Why Enterprise IT Teams Source iPhones Through Solaris

Solaris Wireless, founded 2013, has been serving institutional buyers in this category since the company's earliest engagements. Enterprise IT teams running an iPhone fleet have one specific problem: every device that arrives in a consumer-channel box requires hands-on staging before it reaches an employee. Multiplied across 500 or 5,000 devices, that's weeks of IT labour and tens of thousands of dollars in operational cost.

Solaris Wireless solves this by sourcing through Apple's commercial channel as an Apple Authorised Reseller. Devices ship sealed, auto-enrol into your Apple Business Manager tenant on first power-on, and arrive at the employee's desk ready to use. Solaris has supplied this service to Fortune 500 IT departments and Google's procurement organisation since 2016.

How the Engagement Works

1. We register in your Apple Business Manager tenant

Once your IT team adds Solaris as an authorised purchaser in your ABM tenant, every device we purchase on your behalf appears in your tenant for DEP enrolment automatically. Your MDM (Jamf, Intune, Workspace ONE, Mosyle, Kandji) is federated to ABM and pulls the enrolment payload before the user reaches the home screen.

2. We allocate iPhones through Apple commercial channels

Solaris sources iPhone 16, iPhone 15 and iPhone SE through Apple's commercial sales channel at institutional pricing. For multinational deployments, the Miami, Netherlands, Dubai, Hong Kong and Singapore supply nodes ship from the closest origin point to each employee or office.

3. Devices ship sealed with asset tags applied

Each device is asset-tagged to your scheme (barcode, QR, RFID), packaged in tamper-evident shipping, and dispatched direct to the employee or to a depot consolidation point per your preference. Chain-of-custody tracking is included for engagements that require it (government, healthcare, finance).

4. AppleCare for Business attaches if required

For 100+ unit fleets, AppleCare for Business is typically attached at order. It adds dedicated enterprise support contacts, on-site service for qualifying volumes, advance replacement and SLA-backed repair turnaround. The integration with ABM means coverage and asset records reconcile automatically.

Solaris's iPhone Track Record

Solaris Wireless has been a Google-approved vendor since 2016, supplying iPhones, Android handsets, consumer electronics and IoT devices for Google's internal testing and global deployment programmes across 20+ countries. Beyond Google, the iPhone fleet capability serves Fortune 500 enterprises, healthcare systems and the Ritual.co restaurant programme (covered in the Ritual case study).

Whether you need 50 iPhone 15s for a sales-team refresh or 5,000 iPhone 16 Pros for a global field-engineering rollout, Solaris coordinates allocation, provisioning and fulfilment as a single engagement. Contact the Solaris team for pricing and lead time on your specific deployment.

Selecting iPhone Models for Enterprise

Standard enterprise deployment

iPhone 16 / iPhone 15 are the typical default. Strong device longevity (Apple supports older iPhones for 5-7 years of iOS updates), excellent enterprise app compatibility, and full AppleCare for Business coverage. 128 GB or 256 GB suits most enterprise users.

Field and rugged use

iPhone Pro models offer better camera and battery for field-sales, real-estate and inspection roles. For genuinely hazardous environments, Solaris supplies the iPhone Pro paired with third-party rugged cases (OtterBox Defender, Catalyst Pro).

Cost-conscious enterprise deployment

iPhone SE (when available in current generation) provides Touch ID, A-series performance and full enterprise device management at a lower price point. Suitable for retail, hospitality and large-scale 1:1 deployments where cost-per-device matters.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Solaris Wireless supply iPhones to enterprises?

Solaris coordinates iPhone supply through Apple Authorised Reseller commercial channels. We register as the authorised purchaser in your Apple Business Manager tenant so devices auto-enrol into your MDM via DEP on the first power-on, eliminating per-device IT staging.

What MDM platforms does Solaris support for iPhone deployment?

Apple Business Manager federates with all major Apple-supporting MDMs: Jamf Pro, Microsoft Intune, VMware Workspace ONE, Mosyle, Kandji, Hexnode and Apple-certified solutions. We pre-pair to your platform before shipment.

Can iPhones ship direct to my employees?

Yes. Solaris ships sealed iPhones with asset tags applied to your scheme, packaged in tamper-evident shipping, direct to the employee address. The employee opens the box and the device auto-enrols to your MDM in under 60 seconds.

Does Solaris attach AppleCare for Business?

Yes, on request. For 100+ unit fleets we strongly recommend it. The cost is modest relative to productivity protection from rapid replacement and on-site service for qualifying volumes.

What is the minimum iPhone order Solaris will fulfil?

Solaris Wireless typical bulk iPhone engagements range from 50 to 5,000+ units. 50+ units qualify for institutional pricing; 100+ units receive dedicated account management.
