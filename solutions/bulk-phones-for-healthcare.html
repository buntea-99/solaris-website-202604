Why healthcare procurement is different

Hospital procurement teams cannot order from consumer retail channels. Devices need to be enrolled in the institution's MDM tenant before they reach clinical staff, which means the supplier must register as an authorised reseller in Apple Business Manager, Google Zero-Touch or Knox so devices auto-enrol on first activation.

Add HIPAA, infection-control protocols, and the need for ruggedised cases that survive being dropped between bedside and the nursing station, and the difference between a healthcare-fluent device supplier and a generic distributor becomes obvious within the first 90 days of deployment.

Solaris Wireless capabilities for healthcare

Solaris Wireless supplies handsets to hospital systems, regional health networks and outpatient clinics. The standard package combines OEM allocation (Samsung, Motorola, Sonim, Spectralink), MDM pre-enrolment in your tenant, asset tagging, infection-control casing options, and direct-to-nursing-unit delivery with pre-paired carrier SIMs.

For shared device deployments (where one phone is used across shifts by multiple staff), Solaris pre-loads kiosk-mode firmware that resets the device to a clean state on shift change and can be remotely re-imaged through your MDM if a unit is lost or contaminated.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from buyers

What MDM platforms does Solaris support for healthcare deployments?

Solaris Wireless supports VMware Workspace ONE, Microsoft Intune, Jamf Pro and Samsung Knox. We register as an authorised reseller in your tenant so devices auto-enrol on first activation, eliminating the need for IT to touch each unit before it reaches clinical staff.

Can devices be pre-configured with Epic Rover or Cerner Care Aware?

Yes. We pre-install your clinical applications, configure them with your endpoint URLs, and validate they launch correctly before each device leaves provisioning. Epic Rover, Cerner Care Aware, Vocera, TigerConnect and most major secure messaging clients are supported.

Are the devices HIPAA compliant out of the box?

Devices ship with full-disk encryption enabled, MDM enrolment locked to your tenant, and PIN/biometric requirements set per your policy. HIPAA compliance is a function of your overall configuration; we provision the device in accordance with the controls your security team specifies.

What is the minimum order for healthcare deployments?

Solaris Wireless typical engagements range from 100 units (single department pilot) to 5,000+ units (system-wide rollout). 100+ unit orders receive dedicated account management and priority fulfilment.
