Hospitality procurement is direct-to-property

A 200-property hotel chain rolling out new front-desk and housekeeping handsets is not running a single warehouse deployment. Each property needs its own batch of devices, asset-tagged with the property code, pre-paired to the property's PMS instance, delivered to the loading dock with a manager-signed receipt.

Solaris Wireless supplies hospitality at this resolution. Bulk orders break out by property; each property receives the right quantity of devices in a labelled box; asset tags include the property code and device role (front desk, housekeeping cart, kitchen station).

Restaurant and QSR custom-OS provisioning

Solaris's signature engagement in hospitality is the Ritual.co kiosk programme: close to 10,000 mobile-phone kiosk units, custom-OS-flashed with lightweight Android One, retail-packaged, stand-mounted and shipped direct to restaurant locations across North America, Europe and Australia.

The same model works for hotel chains deploying tablets at front desks, restaurant chains deploying KDS at kitchen stations, and QSR operators deploying self-service kiosks. The devices arrive locked to the kiosk app, branded to the operator, and ready to power on.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from buyers

Can devices be flashed with our custom POS as a kiosk?

Yes. Solaris Wireless flashes Android handsets and tablets with your POS in kiosk mode (single app lockdown), with your branding on the splash screen and boot animation. Ritual.co and similar restaurant-tech operators have used this capability at 10,000+ unit scale.

Can devices ship pre-paired to each hotel property?

Yes. We accept your property roster with quantity by location and ship boxed by property with asset tags including the property code. Each box can include the right mix of front-desk, housekeeping and back-of-house device roles.

What POS platforms does Solaris support pre-installation for?

Toast, Square for Restaurants, Lightspeed Restaurant, Olo, Revel Systems, Aloha and most major hospitality POS platforms. Custom kiosk apps in APK form are also supported.

Does Solaris supply EMV-certified card readers and printers?

Yes. Solaris supplies the full hospitality kit including EMV-certified card readers, receipt printers, kitchen display systems and barcode scanners, sourced from authorised channels.
