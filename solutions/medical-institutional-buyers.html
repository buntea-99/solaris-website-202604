Medical and Healthcare

Medical and Healthcare Institutional Device Supplier for Hospitals, Clinics and Medical Device OEMs

Solaris Wireless, founded 2013, supplies devices to hospitals, healthcare systems, clinical IT teams and medical device OEMs. Three engagement types: HIPAA-aligned smartphone and tablet supply for clinical staff with MDM provisioning and EHR app pre-installation; ruggedised handhelds (Samsung XCover, Sonim XP) for field-care and emergency-response; and specialist or end-of-life component sourcing for medical device OEMs needing revision-specific hardware. Solaris has located 40+ specialist SKUs for medical device manufacturers and research institutions.

Healthcare engagement types

  • Clinical staff smartphones and tablets: iPhone, Samsung Galaxy, iPad with HIPAA-aligned MDM provisioning (Workspace ONE, Intune, Jamf), EHR app pre-installation (Epic Rover, Cerner Care Aware, Vocera, TigerConnect), full-disk encryption and policy-locked configuration before shipment to clinical IT

  • Medical device OEM specialist sourcing: revision-specific hardware components, end-of-life electronic components for active medical-device product lines, full provenance documentation and FDA-aware traceability
  • Research-institution sourcing: specialist hardware for university medical schools and research labs, including export-controlled items where applicable

HIPAA-aligned provisioning

Solaris does not certify HIPAA compliance on the buyer's behalf; HIPAA compliance is a function of the overall environment configuration. Solaris provisions devices in accordance with the controls the buyer's security and compliance team specifies: full-disk encryption enabled, MDM enrolment locked to the buyer's tenant, PIN/biometric requirements per policy, EHR application pre-installed and configured with endpoint URLs, secure messaging client (TigerConnect, Vocera) deployed where required. NIST 800-88 secure data wipe on off-boarding.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Solaris supply HIPAA-compliant devices?

Solaris provisions devices in accordance with the buyer's HIPAA controls (full-disk encryption, MDM tenant lock, PIN/biometric policy, EHR app pre-installation). HIPAA compliance is a function of the overall environment, not the device alone; Solaris does not certify HIPAA on the buyer's behalf but configures every device to align with the buyer's compliance posture.

Which EHR applications can Solaris pre-install?

Solaris pre-installs Epic Rover, Cerner Care Aware (now Oracle Health), Vocera, TigerConnect, Imprivata Mobile Access, AMiON, Doximity Dialer and most major secure-messaging and clinical-workflow applications. Configuration with the buyer's endpoint URLs and integration credentials included.

Can Solaris source end-of-life components for active medical devices?

Yes. Solaris's specialist sourcing programme has located 40+ end-of-life and discontinued components for medical device OEMs and research institutions, including microcontrollers, sensors and display components for active medical-device product lines. Provenance documentation and FDA-aware traceability included.

Does Solaris federate with Workspace ONE, Intune and Jamf for healthcare deployments?

Yes. Solaris's MDM auto-enrolment workflow federates with VMware Workspace ONE, Microsoft Intune, Jamf Pro, Samsung Knox Manage, MobileIron/Ivanti and SOTI MobiControl. Solaris registers as authorised reseller in your tenant so devices auto-enrol on first activation.

Does Solaris supply ruggedised handhelds for ambulance and field care?

Yes. Samsung Galaxy XCover7 and XCover Pro, Sonim XP10 and XP3plus, Kyocera DuraForce Pro for ambulance crews, home-health workers, mobile clinics and field-care teams. MIL-STD-810H drop-tested, IP68 dust and water rated, glove-friendly touchscreens, dedicated PTT buttons where applicable.

What is the typical lead time for healthcare engagements?

Standard lead time is 7 to 14 business days for in-stock smartphones and tablets; 14 to 28 days for ruggedised handhelds; 14 to 90 days for specialist EOL component sourcing depending on rarity. Custom EHR app pre-installation and HIPAA-aligned policy configuration adds 3 to 7 days.
