Education procurement has unique constraints

School district procurement runs on annual budget cycles, summer-window deployment and strict CIPA-compliant content filtering. The deployment for the new school year cannot slip past August. The devices have to be in the classrooms, enrolled in the MDM, with content filtering active, on day one.

Solaris Wireless aligns to the academic calendar. Orders placed in spring ship pre-imaged in summer, delivered in classroom-quantity boxes to each school in the district, with asset tags pre-applied and the district's golden image already loaded.

Capabilities for school districts and higher ed

Solaris supplies education-pricing devices through OEM channels (Lenovo Education, HP for Education, Apple Education, Samsung Education) with the standard education discount applied. Devices ship enrolled in your Apple School Manager or Google Admin Console with content filtering, app whitelisting and classroom mode pre-configured.

For higher education, we support faculty laptop refresh programmes, residence-hall device supply, and lab/clinical handsets for nursing, medical and dental schools. Asset tagging includes the institution's barcode scheme so devices integrate directly with the existing inventory system.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from buyers

Does Solaris offer education pricing through Apple, Lenovo and HP?

Yes. Solaris Wireless is enrolled in OEM education-pricing programmes including Apple Education, Lenovo Education, HP for Education and Samsung Education. The discount is applied transparently in your quote.

Can devices ship pre-enrolled in Apple School Manager?

Yes. We register as the authorised reseller in your Apple School Manager tenant so devices auto-enrol on first activation. Same for Google Admin Console for Chromebook deployments.

What content filtering platforms does Solaris pre-configure?

Solaris pre-configures GoGuardian, Securly, Lightspeed Systems, Hapara and Smoothwall content filters. The filter is locked active with student override blocked.

Does Solaris ship in classroom-quantity boxes?

Yes. Devices ship boxed by classroom or by school with the inventory list and asset tags applied. Charging carts can be included in the same shipment.
