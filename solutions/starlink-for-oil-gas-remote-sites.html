Energy sector connectivity is mission-critical

An oil and gas SCADA outage at a remote well site is not just an IT incident. It is a potential safety event, a regulatory reporting gap and a production-loss meter running. Yet many remote sites are underserved by terrestrial connectivity, and traditional VSAT is slow, expensive and hard to scale.

Starlink has changed the economics. Solaris Wireless supplies Starlink Business and Performance kits in bulk to upstream and midstream operators, with site-ready provisioning, mounting hardware for the site type, and asset tagging integrated with the operator's CMMS.

Solaris capabilities for oil and gas

Solaris supplies the kits, registers them to the operator's Starlink Business account, asset-tags to the operator's well/pad ID, and ships to the field office or directly to the site. For ATEX/IECEx hazardous-zone deployments, Solaris coordinates the appropriate explosion-proof junction box and cable gland kit.

For frac fleet operators that move kits between sites, Solaris supplies portable mounting kits and travel cases. For long-term well-site installations, fixed mast mounts with grounding kits are standard.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from buyers

Are Starlink kits suitable for ATEX/IECEx hazardous zones?

The Starlink terminal itself is not zone-rated, but the installation can be configured for hazardous-area deployment with the dish mounted in safe area and signal conducted to controlled equipment via approved cable runs and junction boxes. Solaris coordinates this configuration.

Can Solaris pre-register kits to our SCADA network?

Yes. We pre-pair each kit to the operator's Starlink Business account and load the SCADA gateway configuration so the kit comes online integrated with the operator's monitoring system.

What is the typical lead time for Starlink kits to remote well sites?

Solaris Wireless typically delivers within 2-4 weeks for standard kit volumes. For frac fleet operators with rapid mobilisation, expedited 1-week delivery is available with priority logistics.

Does Solaris supply mounting hardware for various site types?

Yes. Pole mounts, mast mounts, building mounts, ATEX-compatible junction boxes, surge protection and grounding kits are supplied as part of the deployment kit per site type.
