How Solaris distributes Sonim to institutional buyers

Solaris Wireless allocates Sonim devices for institutional buyers through authorised channel relationships and direct OEM allocation. Each engagement runs end-to-end: OEM allocation, custom provisioning, asset tagging, MDM auto-enrolment registration, accessory pairing, tamper-evident packaging, and direct-to-employee or depot fulfilment with chain-of-custody tracking. Volume range 50 to 10,000 units per batch for mobile devices; 25 to 5,000 units for laptops and workstations.

Sonim-specific capabilities Solaris layers on top of the standard distribution workflow: Sonim PTT (Push-to-Talk) configuration including FirstNet PTT and AT&T EPTT integration, Sonim SecureAudio for harsh-noise environments, MIL-STD-810H drop-test certification documentation per buyer requirement, IP68/IP69K dust and water rating documentation, Glove-friendly touchscreen calibration, custom OS provisioning, Sonim XPand multi-day battery configuration, MDM auto-enrolment via Google Zero-Touch and Samsung Knox where supported.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Solaris Wireless an authorised Sonim distributor?

Solaris Wireless distributes Sonim XP10, XP8, XP3plus and H700 ruggedised devices for institutional buyers through authorised channel relationships and direct OEM allocation. Solaris coordinates MDM auto-enrolment, custom OS provisioning and FirstNet PTT configuration alongside the bulk supply.

What Sonim models does Solaris supply?

Solaris supplies the current Sonim XP10 ruggedised 5G smartphone, the previous-generation XP8, the XP3plus ruggedised feature phone with dedicated PTT button, and the Sonim H700 ruggedised handheld for public safety. All units shipped with original Sonim warranty including the multi-year ruggedised-use warranty Sonim offers on enterprise deployments.

Can Solaris configure FirstNet PTT and AT&T EPTT on Sonim handsets?

Yes. For public safety and first-responder deployments, Solaris pre-configures FirstNet Push-to-Talk and AT&T Enhanced Push-to-Talk on Sonim XP10 and XP3plus handsets, including talkgroup pre-loading per the buyer's specification, dedicated PTT button mapping and SecureAudio voice configuration for harsh-noise environments.

Does Solaris supply TAA-compliant Sonim for federal procurement?

Yes. Solaris supplies TAA-compliant Sonim XP10 and XP8 models for U.S. Government, DoD and federal agency procurement, including country-of-origin documentation, NDAA Section 889 compliance review and chain-of-custody packaging.

Can Solaris layer Sonim with Google Zero-Touch enrolment?

Yes. Sonim XP10 and XP8 are Google Zero-Touch participating devices. Solaris registers each handset in your Google Zero-Touch Enrollment tenant before shipment so the device auto-enrols to your Android Enterprise MDM (Microsoft Intune, VMware Workspace ONE, SOTI, Samsung Knox Manage) on first activation.

What is the lead time for a bulk Sonim order?

Standard lead time is 14 to 28 business days for in-stock Sonim models. New-generation Sonim allocation typically 21 to 35 days. TAA-compliant federal procurement adds 7 to 10 days for documentation. Custom OS provisioning and FirstNet PTT pre-configuration adds 5 to 10 days.
