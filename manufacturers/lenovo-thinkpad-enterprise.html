Frequently Asked Questions

Is Solaris Wireless an authorised Lenovo distributor?

Solaris Wireless distributes Lenovo ThinkPad, ThinkBook, ThinkCentre and ThinkStation devices for institutional buyers through authorised channel relationships. Solaris coordinates Microsoft Autopilot pre-registration in your Intune tenant for hands-off Windows enterprise enrolment.

What Lenovo models does Solaris supply for enterprise procurement?

Solaris supplies the full Lenovo ThinkPad range (X1 Carbon, T series, P series workstations, L series), ThinkBook, ThinkCentre desktops, ThinkStation workstations, and Lenovo Yoga/Legion enterprise variants. All units shipped with original Lenovo warranty.

Can Solaris register ThinkPads in my Microsoft Autopilot tenant?

Yes. Solaris registers as the authorised reseller in your Microsoft Autopilot tenant via the partner-uploaded device-hash workflow. Every ThinkPad ordered on your behalf is pre-registered. The employee unboxes, signs in with corporate Azure AD credentials, and the device is fully Autopilot-enrolled to your Intune tenant within minutes of first boot.

Does Solaris supply TAA-compliant Lenovo for federal procurement?

Yes. Solaris supplies TAA-compliant Lenovo ThinkPad, ThinkBook, ThinkCentre and ThinkStation models for U.S. Government and federal agency procurement, including country-of-origin documentation, NDAA Section 889 compliance review and chain-of-custody packaging.

Can Solaris layer custom Windows imaging alongside Autopilot?

Yes. For deployments requiring custom Windows 11 images (specific application stack, custom security baseline, BitLocker configuration), Solaris layers custom imaging alongside Autopilot enrolment. The image deploys via Autopilot's user-driven or self-deploying mode on first activation.

What is the lead time for a bulk Lenovo order?

Standard lead time is 10 to 21 business days for in-stock ThinkPad models. P series mobile workstations and ThinkStation typically 14 to 28 days. TAA-compliant federal procurement adds 7 to 10 days for documentation. Custom imaging adds 5 to 10 days.
