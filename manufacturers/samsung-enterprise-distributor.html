How Solaris distributes Samsung to institutional buyers

Solaris Wireless allocates Samsung devices for institutional buyers through authorised channel relationships and direct OEM allocation. Each engagement runs end-to-end: OEM allocation, custom provisioning, asset tagging, MDM auto-enrolment registration, accessory pairing, tamper-evident packaging, and direct-to-employee or depot fulfilment with chain-of-custody tracking. Volume range 50 to 10,000 units per batch for mobile devices; 25 to 5,000 units for laptops and workstations.

Samsung-specific capabilities Solaris layers on top of the standard distribution workflow: Samsung Knox Mobile Enrollment registration, Knox security profile configuration, Knox container deployment, Samsung-specific device-restriction policies, Samsung E-FOTA (firmware over-the-air) management, Samsung KME tenant onboarding for enterprise buyers without an existing Knox account.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Solaris Wireless an authorised Samsung distributor?

Solaris Wireless distributes Samsung Galaxy smartphones, tablets and ruggedised devices for institutional buyers through authorised channel relationships and direct OEM allocation. Solaris coordinates Samsung Knox Mobile Enrollment registration in your enterprise tenant so devices auto-enrol on first activation.

What Samsung models does Solaris supply for enterprise procurement?

Solaris supplies the full Samsung Galaxy enterprise range: Galaxy S series (S25, S24, S23), Galaxy A series (A55, A35, A25), Galaxy XCover ruggedised (XCover7, XCover Pro), Galaxy Tab S and Tab A enterprise tablets, and Galaxy Z Fold and Z Flip enterprise variants. All units shipped with original Samsung warranty.

Does Solaris register Samsung devices in my Knox Mobile Enrollment tenant?

Yes. Solaris registers as the authorised reseller in your Samsung Knox Mobile Enrollment tenant. Every Samsung device ordered on your behalf appears in your KME for auto-enrolment. The employee unboxes, signs in with corporate credentials, and the device is fully managed by your MDM (Microsoft Intune, VMware Workspace ONE, Jamf Pro, Samsung Knox Manage, MobileIron) within 60 seconds.

Can Solaris layer Samsung Knox alongside Google Zero-Touch enrolment?

Yes. For Android Enterprise programmes, Solaris layers Samsung Knox Mobile Enrollment alongside Google Zero-Touch Enrollment so the device benefits from both Samsung-specific Knox security depth and the Android Enterprise zero-touch workflow. Most major MDM platforms federate with both programmes.

What is the typical lead time for a Samsung enterprise order?

Standard lead time is 7 to 14 business days from purchase order to shipment for in-stock Samsung Galaxy models. New-generation Galaxy (within 90 days of release) lead time is 14 to 21 days subject to Samsung allocation. TAA-compliant federal procurement adds 7 to 10 days. Custom OS imaging and SIM lock for MVNO programmes adds 5 to 10 days.

Can Solaris supply ruggedised Samsung XCover handsets in volume?

Yes. Solaris supplies Samsung Galaxy XCover7, XCover Pro and other ruggedised Galaxy variants in volume for field-service, logistics, healthcare and government deployments. Asset tagging, MDM auto-enrolment via Samsung Knox, accessory pairing (cases, screen protectors, scanners) and direct-to-employee fulfilment included.
