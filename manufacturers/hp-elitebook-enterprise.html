Frequently Asked Questions

Is Solaris Wireless an authorised HP distributor?

Solaris Wireless distributes HP EliteBook, ZBook, ProBook and EliteDesk devices for institutional buyers through authorised channel relationships. Solaris coordinates Microsoft Autopilot pre-registration in your Intune tenant for hands-off Windows enterprise enrolment.

What HP models does Solaris supply for enterprise procurement?

Solaris supplies the full HP EliteBook range (1000, 800, 600 series), HP ZBook mobile workstations (Studio, Power, Fury), HP ProBook cost-optimised business laptops and HP EliteDesk desktops. All units shipped with original HP warranty and HP Care Pack coordination available.

Can Solaris register HP laptops in my Microsoft Autopilot tenant?

Yes. Solaris registers as the authorised reseller in your Microsoft Autopilot tenant via the partner-uploaded device-hash workflow. Every HP laptop ordered on your behalf is pre-registered. The employee unboxes, signs in with corporate Azure AD credentials, and the device is fully Autopilot-enrolled to your Intune tenant within minutes of first boot.

Does Solaris supply TAA-compliant HP for federal procurement?

Yes. Solaris supplies TAA-compliant HP EliteBook, ZBook and EliteDesk models for U.S. Government and federal agency procurement, including country-of-origin documentation, NDAA Section 889 compliance review and chain-of-custody packaging via approved carriers.

Can Solaris configure HP Wolf Security alongside MDM enrolment?

Yes. For HP EliteBook and ZBook deployments, Solaris layers HP Wolf Security configuration (HP Sure Start firmware protection, HP Sure Click browser micro-virtualization, HP Sure Sense AI threat detection) alongside Microsoft Autopilot and Intune enrolment. The combined posture provides hardware-rooted endpoint security from first boot.

What is the lead time for a bulk HP order?

Standard lead time is 10 to 21 business days for in-stock EliteBook and EliteDesk models. ZBook mobile workstations 14 to 28 days. TAA-compliant federal procurement adds 7 to 10 days for documentation. Custom imaging adds 5 to 10 days.
