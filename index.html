About Solaris Wireless

Who we are

Solaris Wireless is an institutional electronic device supply, provisioning and fulfilment company headquartered in Miami, Florida. Since our founding in 2013, we have built a global supply chain infrastructure designed to serve the precise, high-volume requirements of network operators, government agencies, Fortune 500 enterprises and educational institutions.

We are not a consumer retailer. Every system we operate, every relationship we maintain and every process we follow has been engineered for institutional-scale procurement. Our clients do not order one device at a time. They order thousands, and they need those devices sourced from authorised channels, configured to exact specifications, and delivered on schedule to locations across the world.

Solaris Wireless has been a Google-approved vendor since 2016, and our client roster includes some of the most recognisable names in telecommunications, technology and government.
