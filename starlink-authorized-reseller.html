Starlink Distribution

Starlink Authorized Reseller for Business, Maritime and Government Deployment

Solaris Wireless distributes Starlink Standard, Business and Maritime terminal kits at wholesale to institutional buyers including commercial maritime operators, oil and gas energy sector, government remote-site programmes, NGO and disaster-relief operations, and enterprise multi-site deployments. Each kit is pre-registered to your Starlink service account against the deployment site or vessel before shipment, includes mounting hardware appropriate to the kit class, and ships from the closest of five global supply nodes (Miami, Netherlands, Dubai, Hong Kong, Singapore).

Starlink kit classes Solaris distributes

  • Starlink Standard: Fixed-site terminal for branch offices, retail locations, agricultural sites and similar deployments. Consumer-tier service.
  • Starlink Business: Higher-throughput terminal with priority data and business-grade SLA. Right for fixed business sites with mission-critical connectivity needs (corporate offices, healthcare clinics, restaurants depending on POS connectivity, oil and gas SCADA).
  • Starlink Maritime: Gimbal-stabilised antenna designed for vessels and offshore platforms. Global ocean coverage. Maritime-specific service plans with throughput and priority access tuned for vessel operations.
  • Starlink Performance / Mobility variants: Selected by deployment when fixed-site connectivity needs higher throughput or in-motion use is required (selected oil and gas, frac fleet, government rapid-response).

How Solaris's Starlink wholesale engagement works

For multi-site or fleet deployments, Solaris coordinates the entire workflow: kit allocation, pre-registration to your Starlink Business or Maritime service account against each site or vessel ID, mounting hardware selection appropriate to the deployment environment (mast mount for vessels, pole mount for remote infrastructure, ATEX-compatible junction box for hazardous-area oil and gas), and direct-to-site logistics through the closest global supply node.

Order a fleet of 30 Maritime kits, supply the vessel manifest, and receive kits pre-registered, asset-tagged and shipped via the correct logistics path for each vessel's location. The Singapore and Netherlands supply nodes serve the major commercial shipping lanes; Dubai serves Middle East offshore platforms; Miami serves Gulf of Mexico and Caribbean operators.

Sectors Solaris serves with Starlink

  • Commercial maritime: Container, cargo, tanker, offshore supply vessels with Maritime kits and gimbal-stabilised mounting. Detailed in the maritime solution page.
  • Oil and gas / energy: Upstream well-site SCADA, midstream pipeline ROW, renewable energy substations. ATEX/IECEx hazardous-area deployment configurations. Detailed in the oil and gas solution page.
  • Government and military: Remote-site connectivity, rapid-response base camps, allied-nation deployments. ITAR/EAR coordinated where required.
  • Enterprise multi-site: Branch office connectivity, retail backup, corporate emergency response.
  • Humanitarian and disaster relief: 72-hour rapid-deployment kits for NGOs and emergency response. Detailed in the disaster relief solution page.

Pre-registration and account integration

Each Starlink kit Solaris ships is pre-registered to your Starlink Business, Maritime or Performance service account against the specific deployment site or vessel ID before shipment. The kit activates against the correct service plan on first power-on without your IT or operations team manually pairing service accounts to terminals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Solaris Wireless a Starlink authorized reseller for business?

Can Solaris pre-register Starlink kits to our service account?

Yes. Solaris pre-registers each terminal to your Starlink Business, Maritime or Performance service account against the deployment site ID or vessel ID before shipment, so the kit activates against the correct service plan on first power-on.

What Starlink kit classes does Solaris distribute?

Standard (fixed residential-tier sites), Business (fixed mission-critical sites with priority data), Maritime (gimbal-stabilised for vessels with global ocean coverage), and Performance variants for high-throughput in-motion use.

Does Solaris ship Starlink kits internationally?

Yes. Solaris's five global supply nodes (Miami, Netherlands, Dubai, Hong Kong, Singapore) ship Starlink kits to deployment sites worldwide. Maritime kits ship via port-of-call delivery for vessels at sea.

What mounting hardware does Solaris supply with Starlink kits?

Mounting hardware is selected per deployment: mast mounts for vessels, pole mounts for remote infrastructure, ATEX/IECEx hazardous-area enclosures for oil and gas hazardous zones, ground stakes for temporary deployments. Solaris coordinates the appropriate mounting kit as part of the engagement.
