The Clients

MVNOs With Unforgiving Specifications

Solaris Wireless, founded 2013, has been serving institutional buyers in this category since the company's earliest engagements. Multiple MVNOs from mainland USA to the Pacific Cook Islands have trusted Solaris Wireless for sourcing mobile phones. This diverse group of telecom operators, spanning vastly different regions and network environments, share one common requirement: devices with particular and unforgiving specifications that standard distribution channels often cannot meet.

From Republic Wireless in the United States to operators serving the Pacific islands, each MVNO brings distinct device requirements driven by their network architecture, regulatory environment, and subscriber base. There is no room for compromise on spec, a device that doesn't meet the operator's technical requirements is a device that cannot be activated on their network.

"The spec sheet was non-negotiable. If the device didn't meet it exactly, it was useless to us. Solaris understood that and delivered."
Our Solution

Precise Sourcing, Custom OS & Splash Banners

In addition to sourcing devices to exacting hardware specifications, Solaris Wireless has the technical capabilities to go further. For MVNO clients, we load custom operating systems, pre-install carrier applications, and apply splash (start-up) banners, so each device arrives network-ready and carrier-branded straight out of the box.

This end-to-end provisioning capability is what separates a hardware supplier from a true telecom supply partner. Our MVNO clients don't receive generic devices, they receive operator-ready handsets that reflect their brand and are configured to activate seamlessly on their network from day one.

01
Specification-First Sourcing

Each MVNO's hardware requirements reviewed in detail, band support, SIM slot configuration, hardware revision, and region-specific regulatory certifications. Devices sourced only when specification is confirmed, not approximated.

02
Custom OS Loading

Where required, devices flashed with the operator's custom OS build, removing carrier bloatware, configuring network settings, and ensuring the firmware environment matches the operator's deployment requirements precisely.

03
App Pre-Installation & Splash Banners

Carrier applications pre-installed and configured. Start-up (splash) banners applied, so devices power on displaying the operator's branding. Every unit delivered as a fully branded, network-ready handset.

04
Cross-Region Fulfilment

Provisioned devices shipped to operator destinations across the USA and Pacific, including remote island territories with complex logistics requirements. Each shipment tracked and confirmed at destination.

The Outcome

Trusted from the Mainland to the Pacific Islands

Multiple MVNOs operating across vastly different geographies, from the USA mainland to the Cook Islands in the Pacific, have relied on Solaris Wireless as their device supply and provisioning partner. Each engagement has been built on the same foundation: precise hardware sourcing, full provisioning capability, and the logistical reach to deliver anywhere the operator needs.
