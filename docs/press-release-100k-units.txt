SOLARIS WIRELESS SURPASSES 100,000 UNITS SUPPLIED TO INSTITUTIONAL BUYERS WORLDWIDE

Miami-Based Electronic Device Distributor Marks Milestone Serving Fortune 500, U.S. Government and Global MVNOs Since 2013

MIAMI, FL, May 2026 -- Solaris Wireless, a Miami-based electronic device supplier and distributor specialising in institutional procurement, today announced it has supplied more than 100,000 mobile devices to enterprises, government agencies, mobile virtual network operators (MVNOs), and educational institutions across 20+ countries since its founding in 2013.

The milestone reflects demand from institutional buyers who need more than off-the-shelf consumer electronics. Solaris Wireless provides custom OS provisioning, SIM lock configuration, Apple Business Manager and Samsung Knox pre-enrollment, and direct-to-site fulfilment at scale -- capabilities not available through consumer retail or carrier channels.

"When an MVNO needs 5,000 carrier-branded handsets shipped to the Pacific Islands, or a Fortune 500 IT director needs a discontinued feature phone sourced for mission-critical testing, that is the kind of work we have built the company around," said Bivek Agrawal, Founder of Solaris Wireless.

Notable engagements driving the milestone include:

-- Close to 10,000 custom-OS kiosk phones sourced, provisioned and shipped direct to restaurant locations for Ritual.co (restaurant technology platform) across North America, Europe and Australia.
-- Smartphones, IoT devices and consumer electronics supplied to Google for internal testing and global deployment programmes across 20+ countries as a Google-approved vendor since 2016.
-- Carrier-branded and SIM-locked handsets sourced and provisioned for Republic Wireless and Pacific MVNO operators, including operators serving the Cook Islands.
-- Pre-cleared ruggedised communications hardware supplied to U.S. Government agencies and allied-nation defence programmes with full ITAR/EAR compliance documentation and 72-hour rapid response.

Solaris Wireless serves institutional buyers from 50 units upward across six product categories: mobile phones and smartphones, laptops and notebooks, IoT hardware and smart cameras, Starlink satellite connectivity terminals, consumer electronics at bulk pricing, and specialist or end-of-life components.

The company is headquartered in Miami, Florida, with strategic supply-chain partners across the Netherlands, Dubai (UAE), Hong Kong and Singapore.

About Solaris Wireless
