Supply, Provisioning, Kitting and Fulfillment Services for Institutional Buyers

Solaris Wireless, founded 2013, runs the entire institutional device-supply lifecycle in-house: global sourcing through OEM-direct and authorised channels, custom OS provisioning and MDM auto-enrolment, kitting and assembly with asset tagging and accessory pairing, direct-to-employee or depot fulfilment, and end-of-life reverse logistics. Each service is engagement-priced for the institutional buyer, with no consumer or small-business overhead. Volume range 50 to 10,000 units per batch on mobile devices; 25 to 5,000 on laptops and workstations.

How services combine on a typical engagement

Most Solaris engagements bundle multiple services. A Fortune 500 mobile-fleet rollout typically combines Supply (iPhone or Samsung Galaxy allocation), Provisioning (Apple Business Manager or Samsung Knox auto-enrolment registration), Kitting (asset tag, accessory bundle, tamper seal), and Fulfillment (direct-to-employee with chain-of-custody). MVNO subscriber-acquisition adds Custom OS flashing, SIM lock and carrier branding to the Provisioning layer. Government TAA-compliant procurement adds compliance documentation and approved-carrier shipping to Fulfillment. DaaS replaces the per-batch pricing model with per-device per-month operating cost.

Engagement types Solaris supports

  • One-off bulk procurement (50 to 10,000 units, single batch)
  • Recurring quarterly or monthly procurement programmes
  • MVNO subscriber-acquisition fulfilment (per-subscriber kit shipping)
  • Restaurant kiosk and retail-deployment programmes (Ritual.co model: close to 10,000 mobile-phone kiosk units)
  • U.S. Government TAA-compliant federal procurement via approved vehicles
  • Specialist EOL hardware brokerage (rare, discontinued, export-controlled)
  • Device-as-a-Service multi-year operating-expense engagements
