The Challenge

Field-Ready Hardware. No Room for Supply Failure.

Government and defence procurement operates under constraints that disqualify most commercial supply chains immediately: export licensing, end-user certification, classification handling requirements, and operational timelines that don't accommodate standard lead times.

Solaris Wireless was approached by procurement offices representing allied nations requiring ruggedised mobile communications hardware for field operations. The requirement included hardened handsets, satellite communication terminals, and encrypted radio-capable devices — all subject to strict export control review and requiring configuration to classified standards before delivery.

"When operational requirements change in the field, procurement timelines don't have the luxury of weeks. We needed a partner with inventory depth and the compliance infrastructure to move fast."
Our Solution

Pre-Cleared Inventory, Compliance-Hardened Process

Solaris Wireless maintains pre-positioned inventory of commonly required ruggedised and specialist hardware, specifically to support rapid-response government procurement. This reduces standard lead times from weeks to 72 hours for qualifying programmes.

Our export compliance team holds active working relationships with licensing authorities across the jurisdictions involved. End-user certificates, import permits, and ITAR/EAR documentation were prepared concurrently with sourcing — not sequentially — collapsing the compliance timeline significantly.

Configuration was handled in a physically secured environment. Devices were delivered in tamper-evident packaging with full chain-of-custody documentation from warehouse to final recipient.

01
Operational Requirements Review

Reviewed hardware specifications against operational environment parameters: IP ratings, temperature ranges, battery endurance, connectivity requirements, and any classification-level constraints on software or firmware.

02
Parallel Compliance & Sourcing

Export licence applications, end-user certificates, and import permits processed in parallel with sourcing activity — not after. This concurrent approach reduced total compliance overhead by an average of 11 days.

03
Secured Configuration Environment

Device configuration performed in a physically secured, access-controlled environment. All provisioning personnel vetted. Tamper-evident packaging applied at the conclusion of each configuration session.

04
Chain-of-Custody Delivery

Full chain-of-custody documentation provided for every unit. Freight coordinated with government-approved carriers. Delivery confirmed against recipient manifests with signed acceptance at destination.

The Outcome

Field-Ready in 72 Hours. Zero Compliance Incidents.

All hardware delivered within agreed operational windows. No export or compliance violations occurred across any of the four allied nation engagements. Several procurement offices have designated Solaris Wireless as a preferred rapid-response supplier for future operational requirements.
