The Challenge

Enterprise Procurement at Global Scale

Large enterprises procuring devices at scale face a different set of problems from smaller buyers. Vendor compliance requirements, MDM enrolment prerequisites, regional regulatory approvals, and multi-country logistics create compounding complexity that standard resellers aren't equipped to absorb.

Solaris Wireless was engaged by multiple Fortune 500 organisations — including a major technology platform — to manage device procurement programmes spanning 20+ countries. Each engagement had distinct compliance requirements, configuration standards, and delivery SLAs. The common thread: volume, precision, and zero tolerance for supply chain disruption.

"At our scale, a 1% failure rate means hundreds of employees without devices on day one. Solaris understood that and built a process that reflected it."
Our Solution

Dedicated Account Infrastructure, Country-by-Country Compliance

For each enterprise client, Solaris Wireless built a dedicated account infrastructure: assigned account management, bespoke provisioning configurations, and a country-specific compliance matrix covering import duties, telecom certifications, and corporate procurement regulations.

MDM enrolment was handled pre-delivery where client frameworks permitted — devices arrived at employee desks already enrolled, reducing IT burden at deployment. For clients using zero-touch programmes, we coordinated directly with platform vendor representatives to ensure seamless integration.

01
Programme Scoping & Compliance Mapping

Mapped each client's device requirements against regional telecom certifications, import duty schedules, and internal vendor approval requirements. Built a per-country procurement playbook for each engagement.

02
Preferred Vendor Negotiation

Leveraged aggregate volume across the client portfolio to negotiate preferential pricing and allocation priority with OEMs. Ensured clients received devices on preferred vendor lists without the typical premium for small-batch orders.

03
Pre-Enrolment & MDM Configuration

Configured corporate device management profiles, installed required applications, and where supported, completed zero-touch or Apple Business Manager enrolment before devices left the warehouse.

04
Global Direct-to-Employee Fulfilment

Delivered directly to employee locations across 20+ countries, coordinating with regional HR and IT teams. Per-shipment tracking provided to both client IT and individual recipients.

The Outcome

Frictionless Deployment. Measurable Cost Reduction.

Across all enterprise engagements, Solaris Wireless maintained a 99.6% on-time delivery rate against committed SLAs. Clients reported an average 23% reduction in IT provisioning effort as a result of pre-enrolment handling. Several clients consolidated previously fragmented regional procurement into a single Solaris-managed programme.
