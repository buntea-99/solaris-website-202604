Provisioning Services

Six provisioning services, one order

01
Apple Business Manager Enrollment

iPhone and iPad devices registered in Apple Business Manager (ABM) before shipment. Devices auto-enroll into your MDM on first boot with zero IT setup required per device. Compatible with Jamf, Microsoft Intune, Mosyle and all ABM-compatible MDM platforms.

ABMJamfIntuneMosyle
02
Google Zero-Touch Enrollment

Android Enterprise devices registered in Google Zero-Touch Enrollment program. Devices auto-configure with your Android Enterprise MDM profile on first boot. Custom Android OS builds and Google Workspace policies applied before delivery.

Zero-TouchAndroid EnterpriseGoogle Workspace
03
Custom OS Flashing

Custom Android One builds, AOSP variants, lightweight carrier firmware and bespoke OS configurations flashed at scale. OS loading is performed with per-unit verification. Supports all major Android OEMs including Samsung, Motorola, Kyocera and Sonim.

Android OneCustom FirmwareAOSP
04
SIM Lock Configuration

SIM lock applied to the carrier or MVNO network at the distributor level. Every device arrives pre-locked and ready for subscriber activation without carrier-side reconfiguration. Applied to any supported Android or iOS device.

SIM LockCarrier LockNetwork Binding
05
App Pre-installation

Corporate apps, carrier apps, MDM client agents and productivity suites pre-installed and configured on every device before shipment. Eliminates app deployment overhead for IT teams on large-scale rollouts.

App DeploymentCorporate AppsMDM Agent
06
Asset Tagging and IMEI Registration

IMEI registration, asset tagging with barcodes or RFID, per-unit documentation and chain-of-custody records for enterprise asset management and government procurement compliance.
